Current Research and Writing

I'm currently on a few parallel journeys, somewhere between curiosity and commitment:

  • On music and technology — exploring how new technologies enter the musical landscape in waves, get overdramatised, face rejection, and eventually find their meaningful place.
  • On ethics, AI and creativity — looking at what it means to create, collaborate and make decisions in a world increasingly shaped by machine intelligence.
  • On empathy, values and the human condition — a personal and philosophical exploration of how the stories we're raised with shape who we become, for better and worse.
  • On grief, illness and resilience — a more personal project, still taking shape.
  • In the studio — working across several musical directions simultaneously, from fusion to ambient to found sound.
